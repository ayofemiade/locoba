Frequently Asked Questions

How do I renew my membership?

Premium members will receive a renewal notice via email one month before their membership expires. You can renew through your account dashboard or by contacting our membership team.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes, you can upgrade from Basic to Premium or Lifetime membership at any time through your member dashboard or by contacting us.

Are membership fees tax-deductible?

A portion of your membership fee may be tax-deductible as a charitable contribution. Please consult with your tax advisor for specific information.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for membership payments.

How can I get involved after becoming a member?

After joining, you'll receive a welcome email with information about upcoming events, volunteer opportunities, and ways to connect with fellow alumni.
