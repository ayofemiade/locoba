About LOCOBA

Learn about our history, mission, vision, and core values.

Our History

The Loyola Old Boys Association (LOCOBA) was established in 1975 with the aim of fostering brotherhood, networking, and giving back to our alma mater. What began as a small gathering of graduates has now evolved into a global network of over 10,000 alumni spanning across various industries and sectors.

For over four decades, LOCOBA has played a pivotal role in supporting Loyola College through scholarships, infrastructure development, mentorship programs, and various community outreach initiatives.

Our alumni have made significant contributions to society, serving as leaders in business, politics, academia, healthcare, and various other fields. We take pride in our diverse alumni base and their collective impact on the world.

Today, LOCOBA continues to grow stronger, guided by the principles of excellence, service, and lifelong brotherhood that were instilled in us during our formative years at Loyola College.

Our Journey

1975

Foundation of LOCOBA

The Loyola Old Boys Association was officially established with 50 founding members to maintain connections between alumni and the school.

1985

First Alumni Center

LOCOBA inaugurated its first official alumni center on campus, providing a permanent home for alumni activities and gatherings.

1995

Scholarship Fund

Established the LOCOBA Scholarship Fund to provide financial assistance to deserving students from underprivileged backgrounds.

2005

Global Expansion

LOCOBA expanded internationally with chapters established in the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, and the Middle East.

2015

Mentorship Program

Launched the Alumni Mentorship Program connecting current students with successful alumni for career guidance and professional development.

2025

50th Anniversary

Celebrating 50 years of excellence, brotherhood, and service with a yearlong series of events and a major fundraising campaign.

Our Mission

To foster a strong and supportive alumni community that upholds the values of Loyola College while contributing to the growth and development of our alma mater and society at large.

We strive to create meaningful connections between past, present, and future Loyolans through networking opportunities, mentorship programs, and collaborative initiatives that benefit both our alumni and the school.

Our mission is to leverage the collective strength, expertise, and resources of our alumni to make a positive impact in our communities and to support the educational excellence that Loyola College is known for.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized alumni association that serves as a model for educational institutions worldwide, known for its strong network, impactful initiatives, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

We envision a future where every Loyola graduate remains an active part of the school community, contributing to its legacy and benefiting from lifelong connections with fellow alumni.

Our vision encompasses a vibrant ecosystem where knowledge, opportunities, and resources are shared freely among our members, creating a supportive environment for personal and professional growth.

Our Core Values

Brotherhood

We foster meaningful relationships among alumni based on mutual respect, support, and shared experiences from our time at Loyola.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all our endeavors, upholding the high standards instilled in us during our education at Loyola College.

Service

We are committed to serving our alma mater, our communities, and society at large through volunteer work and philanthropic initiatives.

Integrity

We conduct ourselves with honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our interactions and initiatives.

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and ensure that all alumni, regardless of background or circumstances, feel welcome and valued in our community.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and innovative approaches to address challenges and create opportunities for our members and institution.
