Meet Our Leadership

Our executive committee is composed of dedicated alumni who volunteer their time and expertise to further the mission of the Loyola Old Boys Association.

President

Dr. Robert Williams

Class of 1988

Dr. Williams is a distinguished cardiologist and healthcare executive who has been involved with LOCOBA for over 15 years. He brings strategic vision and leadership to the association.

Vice President

Patricia Hernandez

Class of 1992

Patricia is a tech entrepreneur and business strategist who has founded multiple successful startups. She focuses on modernizing the association's outreach and programs.

Secretary

Marcus Johnson

Class of 1995

Marcus is a corporate lawyer who ensures organizational compliance and documentation. His attention to detail and organizational skills are invaluable to the association.

Treasurer

Daniel Chen

Class of 1994

Daniel is a financial consultant with expertise in investment management and fundraising. He oversees the association's financial health and scholarship programs.

Events Director

Laura Stevens

Class of 2001

Laura has extensive experience in event planning and marketing. She coordinates our annual gatherings, ensuring memorable experiences for all attendees.

Membership Director

Michael Thomas

Class of 1999

Michael works in human resources and focuses on building community. He leads initiatives to increase membership engagement and participation.

Communications Director

Sophia Rodriguez

Class of 2005

Sophia is a digital marketing specialist who manages all association communications, including our newsletter, website, and social media presence.

Outreach Director

James Wilson

Class of 1997

James works in philanthropy and leads our community service initiatives. He builds partnerships with local organizations to create impact.
